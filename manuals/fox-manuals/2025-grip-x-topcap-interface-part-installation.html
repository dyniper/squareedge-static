SERVICE PROCEDURES

2025 Grip X Topcap Interface Part Installation

Jump to step: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5

Supplies Needed

  • 2mm Hex Wrench
  • 8mm Socket
  • Torque Wrench

Step 1 top

Torque HSC Coupler clockwise to the stop, approximately 2 in-lb.



Step 2 top

Locate the peak or flat of the HSC Coupler that is best aligned to the rear of the fork for preferred lever position. Locate the corresponding rear hole location in the top cap, then align that to the peak or flat chosen earlier and install the Stop Pin. The pin is intended to prevent the adjuster from over-rotating beyond the open position adjustment range. 



Step 3 top

Align the lever of the HSC Knob with the peak or flat of the HSC Coupler and installed Stop Pin. The pin is set slightly counter-clockwise from the peak or flat alignment to assure a positive firm position stop at the adjuster. The pin is the stop in the open position and must not be set too close to the adjuster stop or the firm setting will be compromised. Starting with the rear of the knob at the detent ball, install the knob into the topcap. Align the peak or flat hex feature of the Coupler to the hex feature of the knob aligned to the rearward postion.

 



Step 4 top

Check HSC adjuster setup by rotating it counterclockwise, confirming 14-15 detent clicks to full hardstop on pin.



Step 5 top

Install the detent spring and ball in the top of the HSC adjuster and align the LSC adjuster to the LSC Coupler and istall into the HSC adjuster with fastener. Holding the LSC in place torque the fastener clockwise to 4in-lb. Check detent feel by rotating adjuster and confirm 16-18 clicks(4 full turns).
