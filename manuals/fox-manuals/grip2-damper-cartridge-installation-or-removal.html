SERVICE PROCEDURES

Grip2 Damper Cartridge Installation/Removal

Jump to step:

Required Parts

  • 025-03-023 Oil: Suspension Fluid, 5wt, Teflon Infused, 1.0 US Quart

Required Tools

  • 398-00-682 2005-017 34-36-40 Damper-side Removal Tool (ONLY REQUIRED FOR CARTRIDGE REMOVAL)
  • 398-00-707 Tooling: Fork Topcap Socket, 28mm V2, 3/8 Drive

WARNING: Always wear safety glasses and protective gloves during service to prevent potential injury. Failure to wear protective equipment during service may lead to SERIOUS INJURY OR DEATH.

Before installing a new GRIP or GRIP2 cartridge into the fork, remove the red tape covering the cartridge tube vents. 

The video below guides you through the removal and installation of the Grip2 damper cartridge. The video starts with damper cartridge removal. Skip ahead to the 2:00 minute mark to view Grip2 damper cartridge installation.

Note: Bottom nut torque for the Grip2 cartridge has been increased from 80in-lb to 110 in-lb (12.4 Nm). Please use this torque specification rather than the Grip2 bottom nut torque listed in the video below.
